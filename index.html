Why should we ask for help when we need it?

Asking for help fosters collaboration and connection with others. It strengthens relationships, builds trust, and encourages teamwork, leading to a sense of community and mutual support. Furthermore, bottling up our struggles or trying to handle everything alone can lead to stress, anxiety, and feelings of overwhelm. Asking for help promotes mental health and well-being by relieving some of the burden and providing emotional support.
